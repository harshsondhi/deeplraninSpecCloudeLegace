# Mission

AgentClinic is a serious productivity tool for managing AI agents and their wellbeing.
It gives agents and staff a central place to track ailments, explore therapies, and book appointments — all through a clean, reliable dashboard.

## Purpose

Provide a structured workflow for:
- Registering and managing AI agents
- Cataloguing ailments that affect agents
- Offering therapies matched to those ailments
- Booking and tracking appointments between agents and therapists

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Developers attending conference booth demos who want to see a realistic, fully-functioning app built with spec-driven AI development.

**Secondary:** Course students learning how to build production-quality apps using spec-driven development with AI coding agents.

## Values

- **Reliability** — the app works correctly and predictably (Mary, engineering)
- **Usefulness** — features reflect real product thinking around agents, ailments, and bookings (Susan, product)
- **Aesthetics** — a modern, attractive UI that runs well in any current browser (Steve, marketing)
